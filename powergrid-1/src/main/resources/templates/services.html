Our Service Offerings

Leveraging cutting-edge technology and decades of expertise to deliver reliable energy solutions across India

Power Transmission

Manages a vast network of over 175,000 circuit km for reliable electricity distribution. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure ensures seamless power delivery across the nation with minimal losses and maximum efficiency.

Telecom Services

Leverages transmission infrastructure for bandwidth leasing and communication solutions. We provide robust telecom services that support India's digital transformation with reliable connectivity across the country.

Consultancy & Engineering

Offers expert consultancy services for power transmission projects domestically and internationally. Our team of experienced engineers provides end-to-end solutions from planning to implementation.

Renewable Integration

Specializes in integrating renewable energy sources into the national grid. We develop innovative solutions for evacuating power from solar and wind energy projects across India.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in PowerGrid's history of excellence and innovation

1989

Power Grid Corporation of India Limited was incorporated on October 23, 1989 as a public limited company, wholly owned by the Government of India. Originally named "National Power Transmission Corporation Limited".

1990

Received Certificate for Commencement of Business on November 8, 1990.

1992

Name changed to Power Grid Corporation of India Limited on October 23, 1992.

1993

Commenced commercial operations in January 1993. Took over transmission assets from NTPC, NHPC, NEEPCO, and other companies.

1998

Recognized as a Mini-Ratna Category-I Public Sector Undertaking in October 1998.

2001

Department of Telecommunications granted Infrastructure Provider II license (IP II), allowing leasing of bandwidth capacity.

2008

Conferred with "Navratna" status by Government of India in May 2008. Completed 220 KV Double Circuit Transmission line from Pul-e-Khumri to Kabul in Afghanistan.

2019

Conferred with "Maharatna" status by Government of India in October 2019.

2021

Floated offer for sale via Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) route - the second InvIT IPO in the country.
