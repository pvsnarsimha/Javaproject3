Our Transmission Network

With one of the largest transmission networks in the world, PowerGrid enables efficient electricity distribution across India

Extensive Coverage

With over 175,000 circuit kilometers of transmission lines, PowerGrid connects all major regions of India, ensuring a robust and resilient grid that spans diverse terrains and geographies.

High Voltage Infrastructure

Our network includes state-of-the-art Ultra High Voltage AC (UHVAC) and High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) systems, minimizing losses and maximizing efficiency across long distances.

Renewable Integration

We facilitate the evacuation of renewable energy from regions like Rajasthan and Gujarat, supporting India's green energy goals and sustainable development initiatives.
