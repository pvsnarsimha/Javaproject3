Key Project Areas

Our diverse portfolio of projects addresses the complete spectrum of energy transmission and distribution challenges.

2023-2025

Western Region Expansion

Strengthening the grid to support integration of solar energy from Rajasthan and other regions with enhanced transmission capabilities.

2022-2024

Digital Transformation

Implementation of AI-driven monitoring systems, IoT sensors, and predictive maintenance technologies across the national grid.

2021-2023

BESS Integration

Deployment of Battery Energy Storage Systems in strategic locations to stabilize grid operations and support renewable integration.

Completion of One Nation-One Grid-One Frequency system, unifying regional grids into a single operational network.
